About Us

Founded in 2001, Avalanche Koncept provides scientific and customer-focused solutions to water challenges in homes, hotels, schools, offices, and industries, ensuring access to quality water as defined by the World Health Organization (WHO). Over time, our expertise has expanded to include property and engineering facility services, meeting diverse design and service needs. Backed by a team of seasoned professionals with experience across various industries, we are committed to delivering top-tier solutions.

What We Do
